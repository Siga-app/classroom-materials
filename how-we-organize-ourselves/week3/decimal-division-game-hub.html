Before you play, remember the main strategy for decimal division.

The big rule

If the divisor is a whole number, divide normally. If the divisor is a decimal, move the decimal in both numbers until the divisor becomes a whole number. Then divide.

Ask yourself: 1. What is the divisor? 2. Is the divisor a decimal? 3. Do I need to rewrite the problem? 4. Can I check my quotient with multiplication?
