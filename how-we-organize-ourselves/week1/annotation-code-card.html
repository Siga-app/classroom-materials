Number the Stars · 5th Grade
Annotation
Codes
Use transparent sticky notes.
Write the code + a short phrase.
S
Surprising
Something unexpected, strange, or that made you stop and think.
e.g. "S — didn't expect her to lie"
C
Connection
Reminds you of your life, another book, or something in the world.
e.g. "C — reminds me of hiding something to protect someone"
Q
Question
Something confusing, something you wonder about, or want to know more.
e.g. "Q — why can't they run?"
How to use
1
Place the transparent sticky over the sentence that made you think.
2
Write the code letter + a short phrase (1–5 words).
3
Aim for at least 3 annotations per chapter — one of each if you can.
