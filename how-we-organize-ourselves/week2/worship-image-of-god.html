Monday
Created in God’s Image

What makes a person valuable?

Scripture

“So God created mankind in his own image.”

Genesis 1:27
Reflection

A person’s value does not come from power, popularity, grades, talent, or what they can do for us. Every person has God-given worth because every person is made in His image.

Prayer

Lord, help us see every person the way You see them. Teach us to treat others with dignity, patience, and love. Amen.

Tuesday
Rights Protect Dignity

Rights are ways a society says, “You matter.”

Scripture

“Defend the weak and the fatherless; uphold the cause of the poor and the oppressed.”

Psalm 82:3
Reflection

The Bill of Rights helps us understand that people need protection from unfair power. A good system is not only organized. A good system protects people, especially those who are vulnerable.

Prayer

God, give us hearts that care about justice. Help us use our voice to protect, include, and honor others. Amen.

Wednesday
When Dignity Is Denied

When systems deny dignity, they deny the image of God.

Scripture

“For in the image of God has God made mankind.”

Genesis 9:6
Reflection

The Holocaust shows what can happen when people are treated as less than human. God’s truth says that no law, lie, or label can erase the image of God in a person.

Prayer

Lord, make us people who notice when others are mistreated. Give us courage to speak with kindness and stand for what is right. Amen.

Thursday
Choosing to Protect a Neighbor

Quiet courage protects a neighbor.

Scripture

“Act justly and love mercy and walk humbly with your God.”

Micah 6:8
Reflection

In Number the Stars, courage often looks quiet. A choice to help, hide, carry, warn, or tell the truth can become a way to honor the image of God in a neighbor.

Prayer

God, help us act justly, love mercy, and walk humbly with You. Show us how to protect and care for our neighbors. Amen.
